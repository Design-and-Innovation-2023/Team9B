Project Description

The objective of the project is to design an engaging and open-ended educational activity by using
the Curio platform, which is a web-based education tool, and incorporating robots to promote
student engagement. By leveraging the robot's capability, the students will be able to foster
creativity and problem-solving skills in a hands-on and interactive manner. Through the Curio platform
and robot, students can work in teams to solve problems and have an open discussion to share knowledge.


Problem Statement

The team has to create an engaging and open-ended activities for the computer science course.
The challenge is to ensure that students will proactively participate in activities, diversify
the content of topic and align closely with the concepts intended for the learning outcome

Prototype Ideas

Considering that the activities has to be engaging, the team has craft out ideas using the robot to make the classes interesting.
1. Snake and Ladder
2. Open-ended Questions and Answers
3. Racing Robot
4. Drawing with robot
