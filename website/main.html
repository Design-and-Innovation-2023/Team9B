Project Description

The objective of the project is to design an engaging and open-ended educational activity by using the Curio platform, which is a web-based education tool, and incorporating robots to promote student engagement. By leveraging the robot's capability, the students will be able to foster creativity and problem-solving skills in a hands-on and interactive manner. Through the Curio platform and robot, students can work in teams to solve problems and have an open discussion to share knowledge.
